About CodeMaster

Empowering Kenyan developers to build global careers

Our Story

CodeMaster was founded in 2024 by Chris Mboya, a software developer from Mombasa who saw the need for quality, affordable coding education in Kenya.

Growing up in Mombasa, Chris experienced firsthand how talent is universal but opportunity is not. He built CodeMaster to bridge this gap - to make world-class programming education accessible to every Kenyan, regardless of their background.

Today, CodeMaster has helped over 500 Kenyan students learn to code, with many landing remote jobs and building successful tech careers.
